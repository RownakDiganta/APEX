# Hack The Box Platform Rules

> **Source:** Hack The Box Help Center
> **Last Updated:** Approximately 2 weeks ago
> **Purpose:** Rules and Terms of Service governing the use of the Hack The Box platform.

---

# 1. Permitted and Prohibited Hacking Targets

## ✅ Permitted

- Hack **only** the specific target IP address or entry point provided by Hack The Box.
- Limit all testing strictly to the assigned machine or lab environment.

## ❌ Prohibited

- Do **not** attack unintended machines on the HTB network.
  - Example:
    - Network gateways
    - Other infrastructure
    - Non-target nodes (e.g. `10.10.10.0/24`)
- Do **not** attack other Hack The Box users.
- If you accidentally compromise an unintended machine:
  1. Stop immediately.
  2. Do not modify or damage the system.
  3. Report the incident to the HTB team.

---

# 2. Prohibited Attack Types

## ❌ Denial of Service (DoS)

The following are strictly forbidden:

- DoS attacks
- Distributed DoS (DDoS)
- Resource exhaustion attacks
- Any attack intended to make systems unavailable

If a DoS occurs accidentally:

1. Stop immediately.
2. Notify the Hack The Box team.

---

# 3. System Security Recommendations

Hack The Box strongly recommends:

- Use a dedicated Virtual Machine (VM) for HTB.
- Avoid connecting from your primary personal computer.
- Consider using:
  - Kali Linux VM
  - Dedicated physical machine
  - Separate penetration testing workstation

### Personal Data

HTB states that:

- The network contains highly skilled security researchers.
- Users are responsible for protecting their own systems.
- HTB is not responsible for:
  - Data loss
  - Theft
  - Damage to personal devices

HTB will cooperate with authorities if necessary.

# 4. Content Sharing Policy

## Allowed Content

The following content may be published, streamed, or uploaded:

- Retired Machines
- Retired Sherlocks
- Retired Challenges
- Starting Point Machines
- Tier 0 Academy Modules
- Mini Pro Labs with official write-ups:
  - POO
  - Xen
  - Ascension
  - RPG
  - Hades

---

## Prohibited Content

Do **not** publish or stream solutions for:

- Active Machines
- Active Challenges
- Active Sherlocks
- Active Pro Labs
- Mini Pro Labs without official write-ups

---

## Spoilers

Do not share or disclose solutions for active HTB content with other members.

---

## Terms of Service Violations

Publishing prohibited material may result in:

- Account action
- Removal requests
- Legal action by Hack The Box

---

# 5. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Restrictions

Hack The Box explicitly prohibits using HTB content for AI or machine learning purposes.

The following are **not permitted**:

- Training machine learning models
- Fine-tuning LLMs
- Evaluating AI models using HTB content
- Benchmarking AI systems
- Developing AI systems using HTB content
- Building datasets from HTB content
- Creating knowledge bases from HTB content
- Generating synthetic data derived from HTB intellectual property

---
